#IoT and me... Doesn't fit together, yet. Perhaps sometimes in the future... Too much coolnes, less maturity. #jaxcon
— Niko Köbler (@dasniko) May 14, 2014
I tweeted this during last JAX conference while listening to an introductory talk to IoT (Internet of Things). Since now I have no experience at all with IoT gadgets, I don't own a Raspberry Pi, no Arduino, no nothing. But at last, I'm curious. And I'm thinking about home automation for my new house...
As I'm addicted to JavaScript, I googled for a single-board computer, like Raspberry Pi, Arduino or Spark, but programmable natively in JavaScript. And I found: Espruino! Voila! Seems that this is a gadget I will test in the next weeks.
